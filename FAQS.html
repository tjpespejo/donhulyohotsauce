FAQS
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I order?

  1. Go to either our website page, Lazada or Shopee account

  2. Search for the product you want

  3. if via Shopee & Lazada: Add it to your cart and proceed to checkout.

  4. If via website: Proceed to order form and fill out.

  5. Make sure that your personal information and shipping details are correct.

  6. Confirm your order.

  7. Wait for your order. Enjoy!

How long 'til I receive my order?

We commit to delivering your orders within 2-4 days around Metro Manila, and 5-7 days for Provincial orders after confirmation
of payment. Orders paid after 2pm will be processed to the next day.

How can we contact you?

Call 09175009324
Email donhulyohotsauce@gmail.com
Leave a message at our Facebook Page

Where are you available?

We are located at 135 P. Santos Street, Malibay, Pasay, Metro Manila.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept payments to our Gcash and Paymaya accounts, credit card via Paypal, and bank transfer to our
BPI, BDO and Robinson's bank account.

How much is the delivery charge?

We are free delivery within Metro Manila.
Within Cavite, Bulacan, and Rizal, it's P150.

We also ship Nationwide via courier, for P150 for every 3kg.
*Please take note that delivery might have delays because of the current situation.
But we'll provide a tracking number for us to check on the delivery status

Do you accept COD?

Yes, we do accept Cash on Delivery (COD) via Lazada and Shopee checkout.

Do you have a same day delivery?

We do accept same-day delivery until 2pm daily.

What are your business hours?

Our office is open between 10am until 7pm, from Monday to Saturday. We're closed during Sundays and holidays, sorry!

Can I just walk in to at your shop to order?

Yes you can, but if you have specific products in mind, better to at least call for availability.

You sent me the wrong items.

We’re so sorry, hopefully this does not happen. But if it does, kindly take a picture
of what you received and email to donhulyohotsauce@gmail.com so we can immediately work on the correction.

Our return warranty for wrong orders and defective products in 1 week (7 days from receipt of the products)

Our return warranty for wrong orders and defective products in 1 week (7 days from receipt of the products)
